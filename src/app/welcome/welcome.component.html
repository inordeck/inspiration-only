Inspiration Only by The High Order of The OctoMoose allows creatives to collaborate on creating original stories, with each participant able to influnce the direction the story takes next.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Participants are nominated by their peers to participate. Each participant is responsible for the next step in the story. Once a participant has their contribution saved, they nominate the next participant.

We fascilitate collabortation between writers on the generation of original stories.
